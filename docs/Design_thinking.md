# Design Thinking Process

## Empathize
Students often struggle with organizing assignments and remembering deadlines.

## Define
The main problem is the lack of a simple and focused study planner.

## Ideate
Brainstormed features:
- Task management
- Deadline reminders
- Daily planner
- Progress tracking

## Prototype
Created low-fidelity wireframes in Figma to visualize the application.

## Test
Asked classmates to review the wireframes and suggested improvements such as larger buttons and simpler navigation.

## Conclusion
The design process resulted in a user-friendly study planner that addresses common student productivity challenges.
